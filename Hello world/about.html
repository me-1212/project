Online EUEE

A platform where students take Grade 12 national examination online

EUEE is an academic examination administered to 12th grade students at all schools in Ethiopia, including government, private and international schools. The test is administered mostly at the end of Ginbot (May) although this is currently changed due to Covid-19 and conflicts. Test results determine the eligibility of high school students to pursue higher education, and their entrance into university. The ministry is also in charge of administering the Ethiopian University Entrance Exam (EUEE).

The services provided by this system are:

  • Conducting and taking exams
  • Posting and viewing exam results
  • Getting reading materials and previous exams

This online examination system is very important in the process of digitalizing the manual examination system and in reducing foreign currency.
